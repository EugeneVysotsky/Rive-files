F.A.Q.

Everything you need to know about working with us

A Sprint is a one-week creative boost. You get 20 hours of focused design work from our team — illustrations, 2D/3D graphics, or animations. One round of revisions is included. Perfect if you need something fast and polished.

Think of a Sprint as a short race, and a Marathon as the long game.

  • Sprint ($4,500 / 1 week): Quick projects, test ideas, smaller design needs.
  • Marathon ($8,500 / 1 month): Bigger scope, more time, more revisions (3 rounds).
  • Enterprise: Ongoing partnership with unlimited revisions and priority access.

If you are not sure which plan will suit you based on the volume of work, please describe your requests and idea in a short cover letter. This way we can offer you what suits you best.

  • Marathon → 3 rounds.
  • Enterprise → unlimited.
